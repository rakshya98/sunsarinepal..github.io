Itahari is a city located in the Sunsari District in the Koshi Zone of south-eastern Nepal. Itahari was derived from word 'ita' that represents bricks and before it was boosted to the current city, there was a lot of bricks industries in this area, which is now shifted to Jhumka and other small village areas. Itahari is the heart city of Eastern Development Region. It has many tourist spots.

Itahari is an undistinguished town at the junction of the Mahendra Hwy and the roads to Biratnagar and Hile. All long-distance buses along the Mahendra Hwy pull into the well-organized bus stand and there are fast and frequent local services to Biratnagar and places along the road to Hile If you get stuck overnight, the Jay Nepal Hotel has reasonable rooms; it is by the roundabout at the turn-off to Dharan.
